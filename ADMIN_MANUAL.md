# Traamand Admin Dashboard — User Guide

*For office staff and administrators*

---

## Getting Started

### How to Log In

1. Go to **traamand.co.zw** on your phone or computer
2. Click the **Staff** link at the top right of the website
3. Click **Continue with Google**
4. Sign in with the Traamand Google account you've been given
5. You'll be taken straight to the **Dashboard**

> If you see "This email is not authorized for admin access," you're using the wrong email. Use the one you were given by your manager.

### The Sidebar (Navigation Menu)

On the left side you'll see the menu with these sections:

| Section | What you'll find there |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | The main welcome screen with business numbers at a glance |
| **Applicants** | People applying for domestic work jobs |
| **Workers** | Your roster of maids, nannies, chefs, etc. |
| **Bookings** | Customer orders (hires) |
| **Verifier Tasks** | Field checks on applicants |
| **Clients** | Your customers (people hiring workers) |
| **Users** | Everyone registered on the app |
| **Payments** | Money collected from clients |
| **Payouts** | Money owed to staff/referrers |
| **Creator Submissions** | Social media content submitted for payment |

Click any item to go to that screen.

## 1. Dashboard — At-a-Glance Overview

The Dashboard is your home screen. It shows:

**Numbers at the top:**
- Active Bookings (how many hires are in progress)
- Total Workers (all domestic workers on your books)
- Verified Workers (those who've passed all checks)
- Revenue this month
- Total Clients
- Total Applicants

**Alerts (things that need your attention):**
- Pending Payouts — money waiting to be sent out
- Creator Submissions — social media posts waiting for review
- Open Verifier Tasks — field checks not yet done

**Quick Action Buttons** — click any to jump to that section.

**Recent Bookings** — the 5 most recent customer hires.

## 2. Applicants — Job Seekers

This is where you manage people applying for domestic work.

**What you see:** A list of applicants with their name, job they want, experience, and status.

**What you can do:**

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Filter by stage** | Click a stage at the top (New, Screened, Interviewed, etc.) |
| **Search** | Type a name or phone number in the search box |
| **View full details** | Click any applicant to open their full profile |
| **Move to next stage** | Click buttons like "Move to Screened" or "Move to Interviewed" |
| **Schedule interview** | Pick a date and time, then save it |
| **Convert to Worker** | Once approved, click this to create a worker profile automatically |
| **Reject** | Remove the applicant from the pipeline |
| **AI Verify** | Let the computer check uploaded ID and police documents automatically |
| **Send to Verifier** | Create a field check task ($8 fee) for someone to verify in person |
| **Call or WhatsApp** | Contact the applicant directly |

**Pipeline stages (in order):**
New → Screened → Interviewed → Training → Approved → Converted to Worker

## 3. Workers — Your Roster

This is your full list of domestic workers available for hire.

**What you see:** A table with worker name, location, skills, verification badge, star rating, hire count, placement fee, and whether they're active (visible to the public).

**What you can do:**

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Search** | Type a name, skill, or location |
| **Add a new worker** | Click "Add Worker" and fill in the form |
| **Edit a worker** | Click the pencil/edit icon next to their name |
| **Delete a worker** | Click the trash icon |
| **Show/hide from public** | Toggle the "Active" switch — green means visible on the website |
| **View public profile** | Click the eye icon to see how the worker looks to customers |
| **Bulk import** | Upload a CSV file with many workers at once |

### Adding a New Worker (Step by Step)

1. Click **Add Worker**
2. Fill in their **First Name** and **Last Name**
3. Choose a **Category** (Maid, Nanny, Chef, Gardener, Nurse Aide, Driver, Sales Lady, or Bar Lady)
4. You can give them a **Display Name** like "Maria K." (optional)
5. Write a short **Bio** about them
6. Under **Skills**, type relevant skills. As you type, suggestions will appear based on the category you chose. Click on a suggestion to add it. Common skills:
   - **Maid**: cleaning, housekeeping, laundry, ironing
   - **Nanny**: childcare, newborn care, first aid
   - **Chef**: cooking, baking, meal planning
   - (and so on for each category)
7. Add **Languages** they speak
8. Choose **Preferred Locations** (suburbs where they can work)
9. Select **Work Types** (live-in, daily, part-time, temporary)
10. Enter their **Experience** in years, **Placement Fee**, and **Salary Range**
11. Under **Divine Seal Verification**, update their verification status and check off which documents have been verified
12. Make sure **"Active (visible to public)"** is checked if they should appear on the website
13. Click **Create Worker**

## Common Tasks — Quick Reference

### "A new maid has been hired. How do I add them?"

1. Go to **Workers** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Worker**
3. Fill in their details
4. Make sure "Active" is checked
5. Click **Create Worker**

### "Someone applied for a job. What do I do?"

1. Go to **Applicants**
2. Click the applicant to see their details
3. Review their documents
4. Click **Move to Screened** once you've had a first look
5. Keep moving them through the stages as you process them
6. When ready, click **Convert to Worker**

### "A client wants to hire a worker. What do I do?"

1. The client should book through the website
2. Go to **Bookings** to see their request
3. Update the status as the booking progresses
4. Mark the payment as **Paid** once the fee is collected

### "Someone referred a client. How do they get paid?"

1. The system tracks referrals automatically
2. When a referred client pays, the referrer gets credited
3. Go to **Payouts** to see if they've requested a withdrawal
4. Process the payment via their chosen method

### "How do I give someone access to the dashboard?"

1. Go to **Users**
2. Click the invite section
3. Enter their email and select "Verifier" role
4. Click **Invite**
5. They'll get access when they sign in with that email

## Tips

- **Check the Dashboard first thing** every day — it shows what needs attention
- **Keep worker profiles updated** — if their phone number or location changes, edit their profile
- **Always keep "Active" checked** for workers who are available — unchecked workers won't show up on the website
- **Use the search boxes** — all lists have search to help you find things quickly
- **When in doubt, click around** — you can't break anything. All changes require confirmation before they save.
